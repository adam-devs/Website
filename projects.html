C Project, Emulator and Assembler ~ Jun 2021 First Year, Third Term

C Project, Emulator and Assembler ~ Jun 2021 First Year, Third Term

Our first year group project was to create an emulator for some ARM11 instructions as well as an assembler for the instructions. The emulator simulates the ARM binary file executing on a Raspberry Pi, the assembler translates the assembly source file to a binary file that can then be used by the emulator.

IC Hello World ~ 28 Mar 2021

IC Hello World 28 Mar 2021

Since this was my first hackathon I was excited to see what the group could make. With our Charity Block Miner submission, we ended up being runners up in Thought Machine's 'Money for good' category. We created a way for donators to boost their donations to their favourite charities by playing our game. Your score is multiplied by a factor to calculate your boosted donation amount. Where does the money come from? Donors such as large organisations can contribute to a pool of money so that they too can contribute to charity in a fun way.
